Banket Recipe

My family makes banket often, and so I thought it would be a great thing to share! it is a delicious pastry that is not difficult to make.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup cold butter
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 1/2 cups almond paste
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3/4 cup white sugar, plus more for sprinkling
  • 1/4 teaspoon almond extract
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 egg white, beaten

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F, place flour into bowl, add cold butter and mix until crumb like texture.
  2. Make well in the center, add cold water, mix together until dough forms.
  3. Chill dough.
  4. Blend almond paste, eggs, sugar, almond extract, and salt together.
  5. Divide dough into 4 equal pieces, roll into 15 inch strips. place filling along center and roll up and pinch ends.
  6. Place on greased cookie sheet, brush with egg white, and sprinkle with sugar.
  7. Bake in oven for 15 to 20 minutes, or until golden brown.
